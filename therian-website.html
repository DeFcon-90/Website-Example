The Process

From waste to resource
in four steps

Our closed-loop bioreactor system handles everything from collection to output — with minimal human intervention and maximum yield.

01
🚛

Plastic Collection

We accept a wide range of post-consumer and industrial plastic waste streams — PET, PE, PP, and mixed plastics.

02
⚗️

Pre-treatment

Waste is shredded and surface-treated to maximise enzyme contact surface area before entering the bioreactor.

03
🦠

Enzymatic Digestion

Therian's proprietary enzyme cocktail depolymerises plastic chains into their base monomers inside our bioreactor vessels.

04
✨

Monomer Recovery

Pure monomers are recovered, purified, and delivered back as virgin-quality feedstock for reuse in manufacturing.

Advantages

Why enzymatic recycling
beats the alternatives

🎯

Virgin-quality output

Enzymatic depolymerisation produces monomers identical in purity to virgin feedstock — enabling infinite recycling loops without quality degradation.

♻️

Mixed & contaminated plastics

Unlike mechanical recycling, our enzymes handle coloured, contaminated, and multilayer plastics that would otherwise go to landfill or incineration.

🌍

Net-positive carbon economics

Every tonne processed through Therian displaces virgin monomer production, saving an average of 2.1 tonnes of CO₂ equivalent per tonne of plastic.

Our continuous bioreactor process has 40% lower operational costs vs pyrolysis alternatives, with a breakeven of as little as 500kg/day input.

Modular & scalable
