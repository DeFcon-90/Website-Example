About Us

Who we are & why it matters

Therian was founded on a simple conviction: the plastic recycling industry is broken. We built the science to fix it.

Our Positioning

Therian is the enzymatic recycling company purpose-built for the circular economy. Unlike mechanical or thermal alternatives, our bioreactor platform recovers virgin-quality monomers from the widest range of plastic waste — enabling truly closed-loop manufacturing at industrial scale. We don't just recycle plastic. We unmake it, and remake it better.

How It Works

From waste to resource
in four steps

Our closed-loop bioreactor system handles everything from collection to output — with minimal human intervention and maximum yield.

01
🚛

Plastic Collection

We accept post-consumer and industrial plastic waste streams — PET, PE, PP, and mixed plastics — with full logistics support.

02
⚗️

Pre-treatment

Waste is shredded and surface-treated to maximise enzyme contact surface area before entering the bioreactor.

03
🦠

Enzymatic Digestion

Therian's proprietary enzyme cocktail depolymerises plastic chains into base monomers inside our temperature-controlled vessels.

04
✨

Monomer Recovery

Pure monomers are recovered, purified, and returned as virgin-quality feedstock ready for reuse in manufacturing.
